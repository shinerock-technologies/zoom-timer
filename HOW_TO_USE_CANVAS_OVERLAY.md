## What It Does

Shows a **compact timer display in the top-left corner** of your screen that you can share with meeting participants.

## How It Looks

- Small, elegant box in top-left corner
- Semi-transparent black background
- Timer title and countdown
- Color-coded border and text:
  - **Green** - Normal
  - **Orange** - Warning (25% remaining)
  - **Dark Orange** - Urgent (10% remaining)
  - **Red with pulse** - Critical (5% remaining)

## How to Use

### Step 1: Start a Timer

Create and start any timer in the app

### Step 2: Enable Canvas Overlay

Click the **Canvas** button (top-right header, turns blue when active)

### Step 3: Share Your Screen

1. In Zoom, click **Share Screen**
2. Select the browser window with the timer app
3. Click **Share**

### Step 4: Everyone Sees the Timer!

The timer overlay appears in the top-left corner for all participants

## To Disable

Click the **Canvas** button again to turn off the overlay
